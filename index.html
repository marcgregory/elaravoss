[Philosophy]

MMXXV

Artistic Vision

I believe painting is a dialogue between intention and accident — a dance between what is controlled and what is surrendered. The truest art emerges when technique serves emotion.

🎭
Emotion First

Every piece begins with a feeling — joy, grief, awe — and the composition follows. Technique is the language; emotion is the message.

🎨
Color as Language

Color is not decoration — it is syntax. Each hue, tone, and saturation carries meaning before the eye registers form.
