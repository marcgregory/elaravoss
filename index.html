[Artist statement]

The surface should confess the making.

Feeling first

Emotion is the brief.

Every work begins with a pressure in the room. Composition arrives after the feeling has a temperature.

Color syntax

Hue carries the sentence.

One tone interrupts, another steadies, a third holds the eye long enough to change the story.

Material memory

Texture records decisions that words would smooth over.

Impasto, glaze, scrape, and wash remain visible because the painting should not pretend it arrived fully formed.

[Method]

From spark to hanging.

01

Listen

Collect the emotional brief: room, light, memory, scale.

02

Compose

Build studies until the palette has tension and gravity.

03

Work

Layer pigment, marble dust, oil, and scraped marks.

04

Place

Document, crate, and install with provenance intact.
